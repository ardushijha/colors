Our Services

Advertising

Advertising is a form of communication used to influence individuals to purchase products or services or support political candidates or ideas. Frequently it communicates a message that includes the name of the product or service and how that product or service could potentially benefit the consumer. Advertising often attempts to persuade potential customers to purchase or to consume a particular brand of product or service.

Types

There are various types of advertising which are mentioned below:
Indoor Advertising: Clip on board, Sandwich, Oneway Vision, Digital print with Sunboard, Standee.
Outdoor Advertising: Unipole, Hoarding, Led Board, Acrylic Board, Electronic Board, Pole Kiosks, Tradeshows and Events.

In-Shop Branding & Promotions

The consumer when walks in – the intent of purchase is mostly likely there. The challenge then becomes to entice him towards your brand or product. This is all the more important in the launch phase of the product. Colours Media has executed in-shop Branding and promotions in most of the leading markets of the country.

COLOURS MEDIA creative team can create compact display and merchandising concepts and produce the same. The operational team can logistically manage the tie–ups, permissions and set-up multi city, multi outlet in-shop promotions across the country. The largest FMCG and Consumer Durable companies use COLOURS MEDIA expertise to execute multi city in-shop promotions programs.

Product Launch

The need to create an impact and hype– amongst the right target segment– is the key challenge when implementing a product launch event. COLOURS MEDIA provides you with the accurate concept and storyboard to launch your product with a dazzling effect. We also help our clients to generate the right audience to experience the product launch.

Our product launch service includes: concepts, storyboard, set designing, recruitment of celebrities/performers, production of AV films, audience acquisition & management, and overall event management.
